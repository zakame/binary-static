Postanowienia ogólne

What is the Binary.com IB Programme all about?

Refer new clients to our MetaTrader 5 platform and earn a commission on their Forex and CFD trading activity.

How much do I get paid if I refer clients onto your MT5 platform?

You will earn a fixed payout based on the volume of individual trades purchased by each referred client. Please refer to our IB commission structure for Forex, metals, and Volatility Indices.

Is there a fee to join the Binary.com IB Programme?

It’s completely free to join our IB programme. We’ve never charged a fee and we never will.

Account management

How do I register to become a Binary.com IB?

Thank you for your interest in partnering with us. We’ve made it easy for you to apply online. Just fill out the online application form with all necessary details. If you are already registered as a Binary.com affiliate, you can email us at affiliates@binary.com instead. We will contact you directly if your application is successful.

When are my commissions paid?

IB earnings from your MT5 referrals are credited daily into your MT5 account.

How can I withdraw my commissions?

IB commissions are credited directly into your MT5 account. You can transfer funds from your MT5 account to your Binary.com Real Account, then withdraw the funds using your preferred withdrawal method.

Marketing i promocja

Do you offer any advertising materials to your IBs?

Yes, we will provide you with banners, links, reviews, videos, and text ads that you can use to drive referrals to the Binary.com MT5 platform.

Are there any minimum client or volume conditions that I need to meet before I can withdraw my commissions?

No, there are no minimum requirements to withdraw your IB commissions.

Do you offer any tools to manage my clients’ trades?

Are you a money manager who wants to easily manage multiple client accounts? Our Multiple Accounts Manager (MAM) application gives you the ability to view, track, and trade on behalf of all MT5 client accounts under your control.
