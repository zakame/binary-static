账户管理及追踪

我如何才能成为联盟会员？

It’s easy. Just go to the signup page and complete the affiliate registration form. We’ll review your application and get back to you if your application is successful.

我忘记了密码，该怎么办？

不用担心, 您只需重设密码即可。

How can I change my payment method?

Log in to your Binary.com affiliate account and go to: Finances → Payment instructions.

我收到联盟收益的方式和时间是？

Your commission for the previous calendar month will be deposited into your account by the 15th of every month.

我怎么知道我赚了多少？

Log in to your Binary.com affiliate account and go to: Reports → Detailed activity report

What kind of reports can I generate?

You can generate all kinds of insightful reports, including the following:

  • 点击和印象报表: 显示您的命中率和点击率
  • 国家报表: 显示您的点击率来源国家列表
  • My Players report: Displays a list of your clients according to their IDs and the date they signed up
